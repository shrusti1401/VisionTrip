Golden Temple
🕰️ History

Also known as Harmandir Sahib, the Golden Temple was founded in the 16th century by Guru Ram Das and completed by Guru Arjan. It is the holiest shrine of Sikhism and represents equality, humility, and devotion.

📍 Location

Situated in Amritsar, Punjab.

🏛️ Architecture

Lower portion built of white marble

Upper floors covered with gold plating

Surrounded by the sacred water tank called Amrit Sarovar

Blend of Hindu and Islamic architectural elements

Connected by a marble causeway

✨ Interesting Facts

Free community kitchen (Langar) serves tens of thousands daily

Open to people of all religions and backgrounds

Looks especially stunning at night when illuminated

🧭 Visiting Info

Open 24 hours a day

Free entry

Head covering and shoe removal required

Early morning or late night visits are peaceful
